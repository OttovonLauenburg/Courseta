User Manual

1.In order to access any function of courseta, you have to login first.

Create a account before you login.

2.Once you login, you can see all your courses on the "My Schedule" page.

Click any of these courses will bring you to "My Discuss" of that class.

3.When you are in "My Discuss", you can read all the chat history.

You can also send messages by typing in the textbox at the botton green area and click 'send'.

4. You can post courses by clicking the "POST" button on the top right corner.

5. You can search courses by typing any substring of the course's department number and title in the search bar on the top right corner.

6. You can click the "ABOUT" button on the top right corner to access this page.
